Globe (Hero Background)

Component

Reuse the globe from /map

Size:

Desktop: ~70–80% viewport height

Mobile: ~50–60% viewport height

Behavior

Globe loads immediately (no empty white screen)

Clicking a country:

Sets Destination field in overlay

Highlights country briefly (existing behavior)

Drag to rotate stays enabled

Legend:

Collapsed by default

Small “ⓘ Visa legend” button bottom-left

Expands on click (same colors as now)

Overlay Card (Primary Interaction)

Position

Desktop: floating over globe, slightly left-of-center or center

Mobile: bottom sheet (sticky, rounded top)

Style

Background: white

Border radius: 12–16px

Shadow: subtle (no heavy elevation)

Padding: 24px

Overlay Content (Exact hierarchy)

H1

Text: Travel Requirements

Size: Large, confident (no marketing fluff)

Subtext

Check visa, ETA, and company policy requirements for business travel.

Muted gray

Form

Passport dropdown

Label: Passport

Default: United States (or last-used)

Destination dropdown

Label: Destination

Empty by default

Filled automatically if user clicks globe

Primary CTA

Button text: Check requirements

Full width

Disabled until destination selected

Secondary Action

Text link under button: View advisories →

Routes to /advisories

Microcopy (only when needed)

If destination empty and CTA clicked:
Select a destination to continue

Mobile-Specific Behavior

Globe visible at top

Overlay becomes bottom sheet:

Sticky

Can be swiped slightly down but not dismissed

Inputs remain fully accessible

CTA always visible without scrolling

❌ Any long explanatory text
❌ Sidebar layouts

Navigation (unchanged)
